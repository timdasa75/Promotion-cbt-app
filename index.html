Help and About

Learn how to use Promotion CBT effectively.

What is Promotion CBT?

A practice platform for Nigerian Federal Civil Service promotion preparation, with topic drills, exam simulation, and performance insights.

Quick Start

1. Login or register.

2. Select a topic or Directorate Mock Exam (Premium/Admin).

3. Choose Practice, Exam, or Review mode.

4. Complete your session and review results.

Quiz Modes

Practice: instant feedback and rationale.

Exam: timed attempt with end-of-session grading.

Review: study mode with correct answers visible.

Keyboard Shortcuts

A-D or 1-4: pick options.

Arrow Up/Down: change selected option.

Arrow Left/Right: previous/next question.

Enter: submit/next based on mode.

Plans and Access

Free: limited content access.

Premium: full topic bank and mock exam access.

Admin: Premium access + admin panel tools.

Troubleshooting

If content does not load, hard refresh with Ctrl+F5.

If Cloud login fails, verify Firebase settings and auth mode label.

If mock exam is missing, clear filters/search and confirm Premium/Admin plan.
