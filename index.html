Study Guide

Promotion CBT works best when you move from guided practice into timed reinforcement.

What is Promotion CBT?

A practice platform for Nigerian Federal Civil Service promotion preparation, with topic drills, exam simulation, and performance insights.

Quick Start

1. Login or register.

2. Select a topic or Directorate Mock Exam when your plan allows it.

3. For topics, choose Practice, Timed Topic Test, or Study Review.

4. Complete the session, then use Results and Analytics to choose the next drill.

Choose the Right Mode

Practice: best for correcting gaps with feedback after each submit.

Timed Topic Test: best for pressure-testing one topic under exam timing.

Study Review: best for calm answer-first revision with explanations visible.

Keyboard Shortcuts

A-D or 1-4: pick options.

Arrow Up/Down: change selected option.

Arrow Left/Right: previous or next question.

Enter: submit or advance based on the current session mode.

Plans and Access

Free: limited content access.

Premium: full topic bank and mock exam access.

Admin: Premium access plus admin panel tools.

Troubleshooting

If content does not load, hard refresh with Ctrl+F5.

If Cloud login fails, confirm the auth mode label and runtime Firebase settings.

If mock exam is missing, clear filters and confirm your plan allows mock access.
